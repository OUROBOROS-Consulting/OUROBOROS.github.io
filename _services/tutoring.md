I hold a Master of Statistics from North Carolina State University and am completing a PhD
in Biostatistics at the University of Minnesota. I have been teaching mathematics and
statistics for over eight years, working with students from high school through doctoral level.
I am also a CRLA-certified tutor (International Tutor Training Program, Level II).

My approach is direct and adaptive. I identify exactly where understanding breaks down,
work there first, and build outward. I do not teach to the test — I teach for comprehension,
because that is what actually transfers to the next problem.

I work especially well with students who feel behind, students whose learning style does not
match standard instruction, and adult learners navigating technology and the digital world
for the first time. If you have been told you are not a math person, I disagree.
