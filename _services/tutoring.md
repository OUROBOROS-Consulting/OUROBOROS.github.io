I hold a Master of Statistics from North Carolina State University and am completing a PhD in Biostatistics at the University of Minnesota. I've taught mathematics and statistics for over eight years, working with students from high school through doctoral level. I'm also a CRLA-certified tutor (International Tutor Training Program, Level II).

My approach is direct and adaptive. I identify exactly where understanding breaks down, work there first, and build outward. I don't teach to the test — I teach for comprehension, because that's what actually transfers to the next problem.

## Engagement Models

Hourly, monthly retainer, or custom package. Sliding scale rates and scholarship support available for students with financial constraints — reach out directly.
