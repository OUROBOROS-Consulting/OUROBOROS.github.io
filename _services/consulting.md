I have years of experience building technology solutions in unconventional situations — from engineering FedRAMP-compliant ML systems for the national COVID response to helping individuals get their digital lives under control. I don't lead with a particular technology. I start with your problem, then find the simplest solution that actually works — whether that's a custom ML pipeline, an Apple Shortcut, or a folder structure you'll actually stick to.

Design is part of that. I've built design systems from scratch, audited existing ones for coherence and accessibility, and implemented them in production code. In high-stakes environments — public health infrastructure, institutional systems serving vulnerable users — "good enough" isn't good enough and accessibility is non-negotiable, not aspirational. I refuse the tradeoff between accessible and visually distinguished. Your aesthetic — minimal, ornate, editorial, or something else entirely — should emerge from what your work actually is, not from a template.

If you need someone who can talk to both the design and the engineering, and who'll push back when a choice doesn't serve your users, that's a conversation I'm having.

If your problem is specifically about AI workflow design, agentic systems, or integrating Claude into existing infrastructure, that's a distinct service — [Agentic AI Strategy & Integration](/services/agentic-ai/) is the right place to start.

## Engagement Models

Hourly, project-based, or retained monthly. Sliding scale rates and pro-bono capacity available for survivors and individuals in active disputes — reach out directly.
